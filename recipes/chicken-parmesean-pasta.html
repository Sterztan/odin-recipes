Chicken Parmesean Pasta

Description

This chicken Parmesan pasta is the perfect dish to lure kids into the kitchen â€” what kid doesn't like crispy chicken and spaghetti? This recipe makes twice the sauce needed, so freeze half for another meal.

Ingredients

  • tomato sauce
  • pasta
  • chicken cutlets

Steps

  1. make the tomato sauce
  2. cook spaghetti in boiling water
  3. make chicken cutlets
  4. beat eggs and season with bread crumbs
  5. preheat oven to high
  6. dip and cover chicken with eggs and bread crumbs
  7. heat cup of oil
  8. fry breaded cutlets in hot oil
  9. add mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  10. put pasta and chicken with tomato sauce onto plate
